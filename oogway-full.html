THE MODELS

Four lenses.
One verdict.

Each model answers a different question about financial health.

SOLVENCY

Altman Z-Score

Predicts bankruptcy probability using five weighted financial ratios. Outputs a safe zone, grey zone, or distress zone classification so you know if a company is at risk of going under.

QUALITY

Piotroski F-Score

A 9-point binary scoring system across profitability, leverage, and operating efficiency. A score of 7 or above signals a fundamentally strong company worth a closer look.

VALUATION

Graham Number

Benjamin Graham's intrinsic value estimate using EPS and book value per share. Tells you whether the stock is cheap or expensive relative to its earnings and assets.

RATIOS

Key Financial Ratios

15+ ratios spanning profitability, liquidity, leverage, and valuation — each benchmarked against Pakistan sector norms, not blindly copied from US or European standards.
